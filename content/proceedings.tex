\section{Lepton Flavour Universality}
In the Standard Model (SM) the three leptons 
$e, \mu, \tau$ only differ in their mass. 
In all other properties they are interchangeable,
because they transform the same under the $\symup{SU(3)\times SU(2)\times U(1)}$
gauge group of the SM.
This Lepton Flavour Universality (LFU) can be viewed 
as a key prediction of the SM. 

Deviations from LFU would be a clear sign of New Physics (NP) effects.

There are theoretical models to explain LFU in beyond standard model processes, such as lepto-quarks.
